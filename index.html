Hello! Living abroad in Latin America resulted in some of the most exciting and rewarding experiences of my life. (Remind me to tell you about the time I climbed a volcano in Guatemala and roasted marshmallows in lava!) Ultimately, I decided to to turn my love of other cultures toward an academic focus, leading me to earn an MBA and an MA in International Policy Studies.

In my graduate program I realized that there is one skill area that every business, every government program, and every non-profit organization is struggling to find skilled talent for: Technology. Upon discovering Eleven Fifty Academy, I decided that computer coding was the ideal professional skill to round off my education and launch me into a long-term career.


I enjoy being a heads-down developer, losing myself in music, lattes, and solving the latest puzzle the code has presented me with; however, I also greatly value developing community with my co-workers and working as a part of a team. I am especially excited by the prospect of working in an Agile/Scrum environment.

In my spare time I enjoy teaching Pilates, volunteering for the USO at Camp Atterbury, and studying foreign languages. (In addition to maintaining my Spanish fluency, my current focus is Mandarin Chinese!)

I am now seeking employment with a company in the Indianapolis area where I can continue to grow as a fledgling developer. Thank you for taking the time of visit my site, and I hope to speak with you soon!
